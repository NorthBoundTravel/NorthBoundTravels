Adventure along Northern Ireland’s iconic coast

Overview

This 5-day guided tour takes you along the stunning Causeway Coastal Route. You'll visit legendary sites like the Giant’s Causeway, Dunluce Castle, Carrick-a-Rede Rope Bridge, and much more. Perfect for nature lovers and photographers alike!

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Departure: Belfast
  • Accommodation: 4-star hotels
  • Includes: Breakfast, local guide, transport, entry fees

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Belfast, welcome dinner
  • Day 2: Drive to Carrickfergus Castle, visit Gobbins Cliff Path
  • Day 3: Explore Giant’s Causeway, Dunluce Castle
  • Day 4: Rope bridge crossing, Bushmills whiskey tasting
  • Day 5: Return to Belfast, optional Titanic museum tour
