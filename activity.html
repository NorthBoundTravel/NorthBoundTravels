Activities Included in This Package

Giant’s Causeway

Marvel at Northern Ireland’s only UNESCO World Heritage Site. Walk among the 40,000 hexagonal basalt columns formed by ancient volcanic activity, and learn about the myth of Finn McCool.

Dunluce Castle

Perched dramatically on a cliff edge, Dunluce Castle offers breathtaking views and centuries of history. Explore the ruins and imagine life during medieval battles and sieges.
